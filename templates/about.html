About Us

Putting the customer first in everything we do.

UCB Insurance plc is rated B+ by A.M. Best (2021) for Financial Strength. The Company is also certified ISO 9001:2012 compliant by the Standard Organisation of America (SOA) for quality management systems.

  • Our primary objective is to deliver innovative insurance products that work for you, our customers.
  • We are driven to innovate and excel, consistently creating exceptional value for our stakeholders.
  • We are are building from a strong foundation to plan for the future.

UCB Insurance is a member of the AXA Group, the worldwide leader in insurance and asset management with 166,000 employees serving 107 million clients in 64 countries.
